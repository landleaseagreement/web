Land Lease Agreement

Parties of the Agreement

This agreement is made between Vijay Kumar Maharjan, son of Jit Govind Maharjan, grandson of Kajiman Maharjan, residing in Lalitpur Metropolitan City, Ward No. 21, aged 47 years (Citizen ID No. 62338, issued on 2050/04/08 B.S.), hereinafter referred to as the First Party, and Ramesh Ghimire, son of Gakarn Prasad Ghimire, grandson of Thakkar Raj Ghimire, residing in Bagmati Rural Municipality, Ward No. 5, Lalitpur District, aged 37 years (Citizen ID No. 29788, issued on 2061/05/07 B.S.), hereinafter referred to as the Second Party.

Both parties, in accordance with Part 5 (Contracts and Other Liabilities) of the Muluki Civil Code, 2074 B.S., have willingly agreed to lease and take the land under the terms and conditions outlined below. Each party has received a copy of this agreement and affixed their signature/seal.

Subject Matter of the Agreement

The First Party, as the registered owner of the land located in Lalitpur Metropolitan City, Ward No. 21, Kitta No. 367/368, area 31-3-2-0 (Ropani-Aana-Paisa-Daam), agrees to lease the land to the Second Party for vegetable farming and animal husbandry. The Second Party agrees to lease and use the land for these purposes.

Terms and Conditions

  1. Lease Payment: The Second Party shall pay the First Party an annual lease amount of NPR 60,500 (Sixty Thousand Five Hundred Only) for the five plots of land mentioned above.
  2. Lease Duration: The lease shall commence on 2079/09/01 B.S. and remain valid until 2082/09/01 B.S. (3 years). Thereafter, the lease may be extended upon mutual agreement of both parties.
  3. Annual Increase: The lease amount shall increase by 10% each year, and the Second Party shall pay accordingly.
  4. Installation of Structures: The Second Party is allowed to install necessary structures for business purposes on the leased land, including electricity, drainage, and water facilities.
  5. Temporary Housing: If the Second Party constructs temporary housing or sheds necessary for their activities, the First Party shall not remove them for 5 years. If the land needs to be vacated, the cost of construction and any damages shall be mutually assessed and compensated by both parties before removal.
  6. Maintenance and Utilities: The Second Party shall be responsible for land cleaning, drainage, water maintenance, electricity, and other necessary upkeep as per regulations. Upon vacating, the land must be returned in the same condition as it was at the start of the lease.
  7. Subleasing: The Second Party may sublease the land to others under this agreement. In such cases, all terms and conditions of this agreement shall apply to the sublessee.
  8. Prohibition of Illegal Activities: The Second Party shall not engage in any illegal activity on the leased land. If any such action occurs, the Second Party shall be solely responsible.
  9. Vacating the Land: The Second Party may vacate the land only after giving prior notice. Upon vacating, the land must be returned in the same condition as it was at the beginning of the lease.
  10. Mutual Agreement: All matters mentioned in this agreement, as well as any additional agreements, shall be carried out mutually by both parties.
  11. Legal Compliance and Compensation: Both parties shall strictly comply with all terms of this agreement. Any violation shall be subject to legal compensation as per applicable law.
  12. Drafting and Execution: This agreement has been typed, printed, and signed by both parties as a true and correct record of the agreement, with one copy held by each party.
