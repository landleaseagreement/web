Land Rent Agreement

Parties to the Agreement

This written agreement is made between Bijay Kumar Maharjan, grandson of Kajiman Maharjan and son of Jit Govind Maharjan, resident of Lalitpur Metropolitan City, Ward No. 21, aged 47 years (Citizenship No. 62338, issued on 2050/04/08, District Administration Office, Lalitpur), hereinafter referred to as the First Party;

And Ramesh Ghimire, grandson of Thakkar Raj Ghimire and son of Gokarna Prasad Ghimire, resident of Bagmati Rural Municipality, Ward No. 5, Lalitpur District, aged 37 years (Citizenship No. 29788, issued on 2061/05/07), hereinafter referred to as the Second Party.

Both parties, in accordance with Part 5, Chapter 1 of the Muluki Civil Code, 2074 (Contract and Liabilities), willingly agree to execute this land/house rent agreement. Both parties have voluntarily signed and exchanged one copy each of this agreement.

Subject of the Agreement

The First Party agrees to rent out land located at Lalitpur Metropolitan City, Ward No. 21, Plot No. 367/368, Area 31-3-2-0 (Ropani-Aana-Paisa-Daam), registered under his ownership. The Second Party agrees to take this land on rent for vegetable farming and animal husbandry.

Terms and Conditions

  1. Rent Amount: The Second Party must pay NPR 450,000 (Four lakhs fifty thousand only) every year as rent for one land parcel.
  2. Duration of Rent: The rent period shall begin from 1th March 2025 and remain valid until 1st March 2030 (for 5 years). After this period, the agreement may be renewed upon mutual consent.
  3. Annual Increment: The rent amount shall increase by 10% every year and shall be paid accordingly.
  4. Installation of Facilities: The Second Party may install necessary facilities such as electricity, drainage, and water connections for business purposes on the rented land.
  5. Temporary Structures: After constructing temporary sheds required for their activities, the First Party shall not remove them for five (5) years. If the land needs to be vacated, both parties shall mutually assess the construction cost and damages, compensate accordingly, and only then remove the structures.
  6. Maintenance Responsibilities: The Second Party shall bear all costs related to cleaning, drainage, water, electricity repair and maintenance. Upon leaving, the land must be handed over in the same condition as when taken.
  7. Subleasing: The Second Party may sublease the land to others under the same terms and conditions of this agreement. All obligations shall apply to the subtenant as well.
  8. Prohibition of Illegal Activities: No illegal activities shall be carried out on the rented land. Any violation of law shall be the sole responsibility of the Second Party.
  9. Vacating the Land: The Second Party may vacate the land only after giving prior notice. The land must be returned in its original condition.
  10. Mutual Understanding: Any matters mentioned here, or any additional matters, shall be carried out only through mutual agreement.
  11. Legal Compliance: Both parties must strictly follow this agreement. If any party violates the terms, the affected party may claim compensation as per the law.
  12. Drafting and Execution: This agreement has been typed, printed and signed as a true and correct record. Each party has received one copy.
